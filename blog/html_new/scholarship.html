About Educatus Scholarship

In our endeavour to become the true catalysts of the materialization of professional education dreams in India, we bring you the opportunity to win yourself an Educatus scholarship. With limited opportunities each year, we urge you to register at the earliest for your chance to win an Educatus scholarship. Our scholarship procedures have been created to be absolutely prejudice free, which means your own hard work and skills are the only things that can set you apart.

Good Luck... Team Educatus!

How to Apply?

  • 1

    Get your Free Registration at www.educatusexpo.in

  • 2

    Visit your nearest Counseling 2017 Session to test your skills and further information. (Refer to Counseling 2017 menu)

  • 3

    Enrol with the Counseling 2017 participating institution/college of your choice.

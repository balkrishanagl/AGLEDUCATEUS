About

The pharmaceutical industry is known to be the one of the most evergreen industry in the world. Whether it is a boom in the economy or a recession period, no matter what, any day a person may fall sick and will need a drug or a supplement pill which make this industry all-time demanding. But for producing a particular drug, a lot of R & D and cost is put together as a result which the drug need to be protected from getting reverse engineered and reproduced at cheap costs. This brings the need for Intellectual Property Rights which are considered as one of the most important economic assets of any commercial entity or research organization.

IPR can be defined as ideas, invention and creative expressions depending upon which there is a publics readiness to confer the status of property. It provides exclusive right to its owner to exploit its commercial value or delegate the same to somebody else. This online course has been initiated with the objective to provide a platform to the candidates while understand the working of pharmaceutical industry, its business models, research, regulatory aspects and its relevance with respect to intellectual property rights. Also the course is aimed to fulfil the gap created due to insufficiency of current academic syllabus of regular courses in acquainting the student with adequate knowledge about the research activities in the pharmaceutical sector.

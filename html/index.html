About Us

Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI) was established in 1927. FICCI is the voice of India’s commerce & industry. It is the largest and oldest apex business organization in India. FICCI works closely with the government on policy issues, enhancing efficiency, competitiveness and expanding business opportunities for industry through a range of specialized services and global linkages. FICCI's stand on policy issues is sought out by think tanks, governments and academia. 

Know More

  • About Course

    Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) has started Online Certificate Course on Competition Law & Intellectual Property Rights (IPComp).

  • Registration Procedure

    Those interested are required to visit our courses website www.ficciipcourse.in and follow the link for the “Online Certificate Course on Competition Law and IPR” and fill the online Registration Form available on the website

  • Eligibility

    Any student in pharmacy/ pharmaceutical sciences faculty doing (B. Pharm, M. Pharm, PhD).

  • Course Methodology

    This is an online course of 3 months duration. The students would be provided with one module in first 2 months and the sixth module is based on case study. The students are required to study the 5 modules and need to prepare a case study based on the knowledge gained and contents of the course. At the end of third month there would be an online examination and certificates would be issued to the successful candidates.

  • Benefits and Rewards

    We offer:

    1. Certification on successful completion of the course
    2. Internship opportunity with FICCI IPR division
    3. Free Participation in all FICCI IP related events
    4. Toppers to be awarded prize money

  • Course Duration and Fees

    Duration: 3 Months

    Fees:

    For students : Rs. 10,000/-

    For Industry Professionals : Rs. 15000/-
